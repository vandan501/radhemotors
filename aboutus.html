Who We Are

At Radhe Auto Sales, we are not just another garage; we are a family-driven business with a passion for cars and a commitment to delivering exceptional automotive services. Established by Mr. Jay Bareja, our garage has been a cornerstone of the Indian automotive industry for 35 years.

Our Expertise

What sets us apart is our comprehensive range of services. From selling top-quality car parts for all Indian car brands and models to providing meticulous car modifications, we are your one-stop destination for all things automotive. Our expertise extends to dealing in pre-owned cars, ensuring that every vehicle leaving our showroom meets the highest standards of quality and reliability.

Why Choose Radhe Auto Sales

  • Quality Car Parts: We source and sell only genuine car parts to ensure the longevity and performance of your vehicle.

  • Expert Modifications: Our skilled technicians bring innovation and precision to every car modification project.

  • Pre-Owned Cars with a Guarantee: Explore our range of pre-owned cars, each thoroughly inspected to meet our high standards.
